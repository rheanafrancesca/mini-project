Sample Projects

  • Stroke Prediction App

    CMPT 340 (Fall 2023)

    In the course CMPT 340: Biomedical Computing, our final group project involved creating a Stroke Prediction App using Python. We applied five different machine learning models to a stroke prediction dataset and after evaluation, developed an app called StrokeWatch which would determine an individual’s stroke risk.

  • Tumor Growth Models

    Virogin Biotech Research Assistant Co-op (Spring 2021 - Summer 2021)

    During my co-op at Virogin Biotech, I developed an Excel template that generated graphs that modelled tumour growth in mice. Using this template, daily tumour size measurements could be inputted into the Excel sheet to generate a spider plot for their in vivo studies.

    Models generated from the template were included in their 2023 publication “Prophylactic Vaccination and Intratumoral Boost with HER2-Expressing Oncolytic Herpes Simplex Virus Induces Robust and Persistent Immune Response against HER2-Positive Tumor Cells”.

  • Language Learning App

    CMPT 120 (Fall 2020)

    In the course CMPT 120: Introduction to Computing Science and Programming I, I developed a Blackfoot language learning app using Python on the replit platform for my final project and received an A as my final grade.
