Project Name

During my co-op at Virogin Biotech, I developed an Excel template that generated graphs that modelled tumour growth in mice. Using this template, daily tumour size measurements could be inputted into the Excel sheet to generate a spider plot for their in vivo studies.

Models generated from the template were included in their 2023 publication “Prophylactic Vaccination and Intratumoral Boost with HER2-Expressing Oncolytic Herpes Simplex Virus Induces Robust and Persistent Immune Response against HER2-Positive Tumor Cells” (as seen in right figure).
